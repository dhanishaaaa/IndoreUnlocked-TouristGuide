About Indore

The Heart of Madhya Pradesh • India’s Cleanest City

Indore is a vibrant blend of royal heritage, modern lifestyle, and unforgettable street food culture. From the majestic Rajwada Palace and Lal Bagh Palace to the buzzing lanes of Sarafa Bazaar and Chappan Dukan, the city offers a unique experience for every traveler.

Known as the commercial capital of Madhya Pradesh, Indore is not just a destination it is an emotion. Whether you seek spirituality at Khajrana Temple, shopping at Phoenix Citadel, or late-night food trails, Indore welcomes you with warmth and flavor.
